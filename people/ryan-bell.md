Builds systematic trading systems end to end — pulling market data, pricing
derivatives, constructing portfolios under risk limits, and routing the
resulting orders to a broker. Most of the day is spent on the vol surface:
calibrating models to option chains, hunting for mispricings, and stress-testing
the book before anything goes live.

Comfortable owning the whole pipeline from data feed to execution, but the part
that scales worst is research throughput — every new thesis is hand-built. Keen
to hand more of the generate-and-test loop to agents, and to replace hand-tuned
signals with learned ones where the data supports it.
