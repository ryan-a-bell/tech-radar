reconciled by a supervisor. Designs the tool interfaces they call, the prompts
that keep them on task, and the guardrails that stop a confident wrong answer
from propagating. Ships most experiments behind a quick Gradio front end so
non-engineers can poke at them.

Drawn to problems where autonomous actors operate under hard constraints —
trading desks and other high-stakes, many-agent domains — and wants deeper
grounding in the quant and retrieval machinery those systems lean on.
